Tumblr
Tumblr is available as an app on apple devices and android. It can also be accessed on desktop as a website.

How to register

1. The opening screen gives you an option to “Get started” or “Log in.” By clicking “Get started” you can begin the process of setting up an account.

2. You’ll be asked to accept Tumblr’s terms and conditions before moving on. After this, you have to confirm your age.

3. The next step is choosing what type of content you would like to see on your dashboard so it’s easier to find blogs that post about your interests. You’ll be shown a grid of options and are expected to choose at least 5. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, you can click the plus symbol in the top left and search whatever topic you want.

4. After completing this step, you’ll be brought to your dashboard. By clicking the icon in the bottom right, a pop up will ask you to enter your email, password and a username. If the username you entered is unavailable, you’ll be given a temporary one which you can change in your settings.

5. A link will be sent to your email, asking you to verify your email address. Once you have done so, your account is ready to go.

Tools

On Tumblr, a user’s primary use of the platform is to create posts on their blogs with differing formats. Tumblr provides seven post formats that can be found on their blog’s dashboard. All post formats are opened by clicking its respective symbol on the dash or through the post button at the top right.



Text post: The first and most basic tool is a text post which is found on all blogging platforms. These involve creating a post of mainly text. Firstly a title is given to the top of the post and the following section is for the main text content of the post with the included ability to integrate images, videos and GIFs into this section. These posts are flexible and can be used for a multitude of tasks.



Photo post: The next post format is a photo post which involves choosing an image(s). Photos can either uploaded from your device or from the internet by pasting a URL. These image(s) are then accompanied by a caption section in which you can type a few words about the image. This format can be used to showcase work such as artwork or photography.



Quote post: The next posting format is the quote post which makes a large font quote the primary content followed by a "source" section to source your quote or add extra information. This format is not commonly used and does not have a lot of academic uses.



Link post: A link post allows users to share a link as their main content for the post while also giving the link a personalised/external title and summary. Tumblr also allows users to add a description to link posts, similar to most of the other post formats. This can be used to fluidly link to external websites directly from a blog.



Chat post: Chat posts are used to transcribe a conversation into a blog post. This is accomplished through the automatic formatting applied to the text in the post. Following the title, all content in the main section of the post receives this formatting. All text on a line before a colon is bolded and text that follows is left normal. This format is mainly for comedic or documentary purposes and thus has little academic use.



Audio post: An audio post integrates an audio track as the primary content of the post. This audio track is either taken directly through Tumblr’s search engine which sources from Spotify or Soundcloud, by pasting an audio track's URL into Tumblr or by manually uploading audio from your device. Users can also add a text caption to the audio with integrated images/videos/GIFs. This post can be used to share songs, podcasts or any other audio one would wish to share as long as it does not violate copyright law or Tumblr’s Terms and Conditions.



Video post: The final post format is a video post which integrates a video as the primary content of the post. Videos can be sourced through URLs or uploaded directly from a user's device. Videos uploaded through URLs are fully integrated into the post if the URLs source is compatible, such as, videos from YouTube. Similar to an audio post this format could be used to share any type of video content as long as it does not violate copyright or Terms and Conditions.


Safety
Below is some information regarding safety while using Tumblr.


○ The digital age of consent in Ireland is 16 years of age. The consent of a parent or a guardian is required to register on Tumblr if the user is under 16.

○ Tumblr also has a setting that make your account more secure. By activating “secure Sockets Layer", it creates a link between a browser and a web server.

○ All blogs are set on "public" by default. However, this setting can be changed to private on a second blog.

○ Users can block other users from interacting with them if they wish.

○ Tumblr offers Two-Factor Authentication. If this is turned on via a user's account, it will send them a code through a text message that must be entered in order to log in.

○ Blogs on Tumblr are not screened or examined by their Headquarters. This means that anyone can encounter unpleasant content and other explicit material.

○ Tumblr lacks limitations or rules in the content that is shared. Due to this, users must be careful as they could come across unwanted explicit content.

Teaching & learning

Tumblr is a content sharing application which makes it a resourceful tool for people to engage with professional work and to learn more about how others view and work.

There are many teachers that have Tumblr blogs dedicated to teaching. Some examples are 187 Days of Teaching, Classroom Collective Ideas and Teaching With Tumblr

Tumblr also invites people to learn about not only new and exciting art forms or styles but also about people's opinions of those styles and forms. Users are able to comment their thoughts under posts which allows an opportunity for people to discuss different topics and share their thoughts. Users can expand their knowledge and way of thinking by listening to other people's thoughts and opinions.
