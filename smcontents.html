Title Description
Tumblr Tumblr is a microblogging platform that allows its users to publish multimedia blog posts. Posts can be shared through the act of "reblogging."
Twitter Twitter is a platform that allows you to post short messages known as “tweets." It allows you to like other users' tweets and share them by "retweeting" or "quoting" their posts onto your own profile.
Instagram Instagram is a networking service that allows users to share pictures and videos onto their own profiles for other users to see. Users can interact by liking and commenting on each other's posts.
Tiktok Tiktok is platform that allows users to create short lip-syncing, comedy and talent videos. Other users can interact with your videos by liking, commenting or "dueting."
Snapchat Snapchat is a mobile messaging app that allows user to send and receive videos and photos from their friends. Users can also post to their ‘story’ which is available for all of their friends to see for 24 hours.
