This website was created to introduce and showcase the ins and outs of Tumblr, Twitter, Instagram, Tiktok and Snapchat as well as some of its terms of service but most importantly, how it can be used for teaching and learning in colleges.

By digesting the information outlined in this website, I hope that you will discover new ways to incorporate the use of social media into education.

Social media has widely consumed our generation and will only continue to do so, so why not use it to our educational advantage?

"We need to bring learning to people instead of people to learning."
- Elliot Masie.
